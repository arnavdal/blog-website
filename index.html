Places You Must Visit.

Panera Bread

I had visited it 18 December.

Panera Bread is an American chain store of bakery-café fast casual restaurants with over 2,000 locations, all of which are in the United States and Canada. Its headquarters are in Sunset Hills, Missouri. The company operates as Saint Louis Bread Company in the Greater St. Louis area, where it has over 100 locations. Offerings include bakery items, pasta, salads, sandwiches, soups, and specialty drinks. As of 2020, the menu also includes flatbread pizzas. Until 1999 and again from 2017 to 2021, the company also owned Au Bon Pain. Panera Bread is itself owned by JAB Holding Company, which is, in turn, owned by the Reimann family of Germany. Panera offers a wide array of pastries and baked goods, such as bagels, brownies, cookies, croissants, muffins, and scones. These, along with Panera's artisan breads, are typically baked before dawn by an on-staff baker. Aside from the bakery section, Panera has a regular menu for dine-in or takeout including flatbreads, pizzas, warm grain bowls, panini, pasta, salads, sandwiches, side choices, and soups, as well as coffee, espresso drinks, frozen drinks, fruit smoothies, hot chocolate, iced drinks, lattes, lemonade, and tea. Panera was once the largest provider of free Wi-Fi hotspots in the United States

Core Coffee Shop

I had visited it 15 March

Core Coffee is a locally-based and wellness-centric coffee shop and café right inside of Carolina Core Wellness. A full-service espresso and coffee bar offering: freshly brewed drip coffee iced coffee cold brew seasonally crafted lattes specialty beverages A variety of healthy snacks and bites from local vendors including Fortuna Coffee, Village Juice & Kitchen, and Bee Healthy Bars & Balls Our coffee shop is located just inside the lobby of Carolina Core Wellness, a community of wellness providers and services that complement one another and serve all 4 Pillars of Wellness mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual, in a welcoming, comfortable environment.

Barbie
great movie

The film centers around Barbie, voiced by a charismatic and talented actress, as she embarks on a thrilling adventure in a magical world. The animation is a visual treat, with vibrant colors and attention to detail, making it an absolute joy to watch. From the whimsical landscapes to the meticulously designed Barbie dolls and their accessories, the animation breathes life into the beloved toys, making them leap off the screen with charm and charisma.


Pokémon the Movie: The Power of Us
excellent

By far the best Pokémon movie, where Ash plays the role he was always meant to: as a living motivational poster who encourages the development of the characters around him. His relationship with Pikachu and all the Pokémon around him helps the characters realize the emotional power that they can draw from bonds with Pokémon, hence the title of the movie. Ash's unbridled optimism and faith in the good will of others inspires the other characters to place the trust in each other, in Pokémon, and in themselves. The pervasive idea that collective power comes from the power of each individual is an inspiring message that few kids' movies meaningfully convey. I highly recommend this movie to anyone who is feeling powerless and alone, and afraid to place their trust in others and be their true selves.
